Fastfeed

FastFeed combines multiple social media connections into one app, letting you see what's important to you in a single feed.

Goals

  1. Curate your social media feed to stop endless scrolling
  2. Follow topics you care about easily
  3. Shut down negativity in the press of a button
  4. Restrict how much data social media platforms can collect
